To renew a driver’s license in the UAE, you typically need to provide the following documents and fulfill certain requirements:

Valid Emirates ID (original and sometimes a copy).
Completed eye test from an authorized optical center.
Payment of any outstanding traffic fines.
Additional documents may be required for specific categories, such as diplomats, people of determination, and Emirati nationals.

Renewal can be done online via the RTA website, Dubai Drive App, or at RTA service centers. Make sure to check if your specific emirate or category (e.g., diplomat) has any extra requirements12.
