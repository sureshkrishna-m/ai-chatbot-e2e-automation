Required Documents for Renewing a Driver’s License in the UAE

To renew your driver’s license in the UAE, you generally need to prepare the following documents and fulfill certain requirements:

Valid Emirates ID (original and sometimes a copy may be required).
Completed and passed eye test from an authorized optical center.
Payment of any outstanding traffic fines (must be cleared before renewal).
Additional documents may be required for:
Diplomats
People of Determination (individuals with disabilities)
Emirati nationals (sometimes specific documentation is requested).
Additional Notes
Renewal can be completed online (e.g., via the RTA website or Dubai Drive App) or at RTA service centers.
For applicants under 21 years old, the process and fees may differ slightly.
Always check with your local emirate’s traffic authority for any emirate-specific requirements.
