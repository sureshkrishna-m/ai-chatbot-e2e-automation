Required Documents for UAE Driver’s License Renewal

To renew your driver’s license in the UAE, you will typically need the following documents and to complete certain steps:

Valid Emirates ID

You must present your original Emirates ID card.

Current (Expiring/Expired) UAE Driving License

Bring your existing UAE driving license for renewal.

Eye Test Certificate

You are required to undergo and pass an eye test at an RTA-approved eye testing center. The results will be updated directly in the RTA system by the testing center.

Payment of Outstanding Fines

All outstanding traffic fines must be settled before you can proceed with the renewal.

Passport-sized Photograph

While not always required for online renewals, some service centers may request a recent passport-sized photo. It’s advisable to have one ready, especially if renewing in person.

Additional Documents (if applicable)

Special categories such as diplomats, people of determination, or Emirati nationals may be asked for additional supporting documents.
Additional Notes
The renewal process can be completed online via the RTA website, Dubai Drive App, or at RTA service centers.
A UAE Pass account and an RTA account are needed for online applications.
After renewal, a temporary digital license is issued until your new physical license is ready.
