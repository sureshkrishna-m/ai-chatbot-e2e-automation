Required Documents for UAE Driver’s License Renewal

To renew your driver’s license in the UAE, you will generally need the following documents and fulfill these requirements:

Valid Emirates ID

This serves as your primary identification and is required for all residents.

Current UAE Driving License

You must present your existing (expiring or expired) UAE driving license.

Eye Test Result

An eye test must be conducted at an RTA-approved optician or eye testing center. The results are uploaded directly to the RTA system.

Passport-Size Photograph

The photo should have a white background and comply with UAE standards (no headgear unless for religious reasons).

Residency Visa Copy

Some sources mention this may be required, especially for expatriates.

No Objection Certificate (NOC) from Employer/Sponsor

This is only occasionally required, typically if your employment or sponsorship status is relevant to your driving needs.

Payment of Outstanding Fines

Any traffic fines must be settled before renewal.
Additional Notes
The process can be completed online via the RTA website, Dubai Drive App, or at RTA service centers.
For diplomats, people of determination, and Emirati nationals, additional documents may be required.
After submitting your documents and completing the process, a temporary license may be issued until the new one is ready.
